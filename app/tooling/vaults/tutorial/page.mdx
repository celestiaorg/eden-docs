### Configure Your Vault

Before users can deposit, your vault needs two critical configurations:

1. **Supply Cap** - Maximum amount of assets the vault can hold (risk management)
2. **Supply Queue** - Ordered list of markets where deposits will be allocated

<ConfigureVault />

**Understanding Supply Caps**

Supply caps protect depositors by limiting exposure to any single market. As the vault curator, you set caps based on your risk assessment. Higher caps = higher risk but potentially higher yield.

The configuration process has two steps:

1. **Submit Cap**: Propose a new cap (subject to timelock)
2. **Accept Cap**: Accept the pending cap (after timelock expires)

Since we set timelock=0 for testing, you can submit and accept immediately.

**Understanding Supply Queue**

The **supply queue** is an ordered list of markets. When users deposit, the vault automatically:

1. Allocates funds to the first market in the queue (up to its cap)
2. If that market is full, moves to the next market
3. Continues until all funds are allocated or the queue is exhausted

For this tutorial, we're using a single market with these parameters:

- **Loan Token**: fakeUSD (what lenders supply)
- **Collateral Token**: fakeTIA (what borrowers provide)
- **LLTV**: 86% (loan-to-value ratio)
- **Oracle**: Mock oracle for testing

### Deposit to Your Vault

With fakeUSD tokens in your wallet, you're ready to deposit into your vault and receive vault shares in return.

<DepositToVault />

**ERC-4626 Deposit Mechanics**

When you deposit assets into an ERC-4626 vault, here's what happens:

1. **Approval**: First, you approve the vault to spend your fakeUSD
2. **Deposit**: The vault pulls your fakeUSD and mints you shares
3. **Shares**: Your shares represent ownership in the vault's total assets

**Share Price Calculation**

The share price is determined by:

```
sharePrice = totalAssets / totalSupply
```

- On the **first deposit**, the ratio is typically 1:1 (100 fakeUSD → 100 shares)
- As **interest accrues**, totalAssets grows but totalSupply stays constant
- Your shares become **more valuable** over time

**Example:**

- You deposit 100 fakeUSD → receive 100 shares
- Vault earns 10 fakeUSD in interest → totalAssets = 110, totalSupply = 100
- Share price = 110 / 100 = 1.10 fakeUSD per share
- Your 100 shares are now worth 110 fakeUSD!

### Observe the Results

Watch your vault's metrics update in real-time. The stats auto-refresh every 10 seconds:

<VaultStats />

**Key Metrics Explained**

- **Total Assets**: Total fakeUSD controlled by the vault (increases as interest accrues)
- **Total Supply**: Total vault shares issued to all depositors (only changes on deposit/withdraw)
- **Share Price**: Current value of each share (totalAssets / totalSupply)
- **Your Shares**: Your portion of the vault, and its current value in fakeUSD

**How Yield Accrues**

Your vault earns yield through the underlying Morpho Blue market:

1. **Lenders supply** fakeUSD to the market
2. **Borrowers borrow** fakeUSD by providing fakeTIA collateral
3. **Interest accrues** on borrowed amounts
4. **Interest flows back** to lenders (including your vault)
5. **Share price increases** as totalAssets grows

The beauty of ERC-4626: **you don't need to do anything**. Just hold your shares, and they automatically appreciate as the vault earns yield.

## Next Steps

Ready to take your vault further? Here are some ideas:

### Add More Markets

Diversify risk by enabling multiple lending markets with different:

- **Collateral types**: ETH, BTC, TIA, etc.
- **LTV ratios**: Conservative (80%) vs aggressive (95%)
- **Risk profiles**: Stable vs volatile assets

To add markets:

1. Submit and accept a new supply cap for each market
2. Update the supply queue with your preferred order
3. Higher priority markets fill first

### Implement Advanced Features

- **Withdrawal queue**: Handle markets with low liquidity
- **Performance fees**: Take a cut of yields
- **Deposit fees**: Discourage short-term deposits
- **Timelock**: Add governance delays for production
- **Multi-sig**: Use a multi-signature wallet as owner

### Monitor Performance

Track your vault's key metrics:

- **APY**: Weighted average across all markets
- **Utilization**: How much capital is actively earning
- **Idle assets**: Capital waiting for market allocation
- **Risk metrics**: Health factors of underlying positions

## Going to Production

Deploying on mainnet? Here's what changes:

### Use Real Assets

Replace test tokens with production assets:

- **fakeUSD** → **USDC**, **DAI**, **USDT**
- **Collateral** → **WETH**, **WBTC**, real TIA

### Implement Security

1. **Audits**: Get professional security audits
2. **Timelock**: Use 24-48 hour delays for governance actions
3. **Multi-sig**: Use a 3-of-5 or higher threshold for ownership
4. **Monitoring**: Set up alerts for unusual activity
5. **Insurance**: Consider smart contract insurance

### Curate Markets Carefully

Production market selection requires due diligence:

- **Oracle quality**: Use Chainlink or Pyth, not test oracles
- **LTV ratios**: Verify against historical liquidation data
- **Collateral risk**: Assess volatility and liquidity
- **IRM validation**: Ensure interest rate models are battle-tested

### Legal & Compliance

Depending on your jurisdiction:

- **KYC/AML**: May be required for certain users
- **Securities law**: Understand if vault shares are securities
- **Tax reporting**: Provide necessary documentation to users
- **Terms of service**: Clear legal agreements

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Wrong network" error**

- Ensure your wallet is connected to Eden Testnet (Chain ID: 3735928814)
- Add the network manually if it's not in your wallet

**"Insufficient funds" when deploying**

- Get more testnet TIA from the [faucet](https://faucet-eden-testnet.binarybuilders.services)
- Each transaction requires a small amount of gas

**"Insufficient allowance" when depositing**

- Click the "Approve" button before "Deposit"
- Wait for the approval transaction to confirm
- Then retry the deposit

**"Cooldown active" when minting tokens**

- The faucet has a 60-second cooldown per address per token
- Wait for the cooldown to expire, or use a different wallet

**Vault metrics showing 0**

- Make sure you've completed all previous steps
- Check that transactions have confirmed (view on explorer)
- Refresh the page if metrics don't update
