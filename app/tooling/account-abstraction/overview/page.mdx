Account Abstraction (AA) is about making your app easier to use. When you use on-chain contracts, you can use familiar web2 patterns with great toolkits.

### What does AA have to do with UX?

- **Onboarding:** sign in without a seed (embedded wallets with passkeys/MPC) → wallets that are instantly recoverable and don't need any extra steps.
- **Actions vs. transactions:** Put multiple calls into one "action" with a clear purpose and one confirmation.
- **Fees:** paymasters can either pay for gas or let users pay with tokens. Make sure to show the exact costs up front.
- **Safety rails:** make rules for spending limits, allowlists, and session keys to cut down on "sign everything" prompts.
- **Recovery and devices:** linking devices and recovering accounts become features of the product instead of support tickets.
