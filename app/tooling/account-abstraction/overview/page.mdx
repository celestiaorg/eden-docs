Eden is moving away from the old account abstraction docs and toward **app-based gas sponsorship**
and **batch transactions** shipped with Eden v0.4.0.

## What changed

- ZeroDev and Privy-specific setup guides have been removed.
- App-based gas sponsorship replaces the previous AA-oriented approach.
- Batch transactions are available in Eden v0.4.0.
- Osaka-related updates should be added here once the final public guidance is ready.
