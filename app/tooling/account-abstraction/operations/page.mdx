Instead of designing for raw transactions, design for **operations** (what the user intends). In order for users to confirm **once** and proceed, an operation can conceal fees through sponsorship or token gas, bundle several steps, and use a session key to prevent repeated prompts. Operations are the heart of the UX improvements Account Abstraction brings.

<Callout type="info">
  Under the hood, operations are powered by Account Abstraction (ERC-4337). Your app creates a
  signed request, a bundler submits it to the EntryPoint contract, and the smart account executes
  your batched calls. We’ll keep those details out of the UI.
</Callout>

## What makes an operation?

**One confirmation, many steps:** such as _approve + swap_, _mint + list_, and _wrap + stake_.

- **Optional sponsorship:** pay token-denominated fees or network fees.
  To prevent users from signing each click, use a **session key**.
  **First-touch deploy:** If the smart account hasn't been deployed yet, deploy it and follow the same procedure.
  **Deterministic preview:** displays the smart account address _prior to_ its on-chain existence.

## UX flow (recommended)

<Steps>
### Compose the action

- Collect inputs (amounts, recipient, slippage).
- If the account is counterfactual, **preview the address**.

### Human-readable preview

- Show _what will happen_ (tokens in/out, contract names, recipients).
- Flag risks (large approvals, high slippage, unknown recipients).

### Confirm once

- Optional: let users **“remember this session”** (create a short-lived session key).

### Submit & track

- Show states: **Submitted → Processing → Included**.
- Provide a single status component with retry/cancel when appropriate.

### Receipt & next step

- Link to explorer when included.
- Suggest the next action (e.g., “List now”, “Share”, “Stake more”).

</Steps>

## Reusable patterns

- **Approve + Action in one go**
  Replace two prompts with one batched call; cap approval to the needed amount.
- **Mint + List**
  Create, then immediately list on a marketplace in one operation.
- **Wrap + Stake**
  Wrap native → stake contract; show a single preview of the final position.
- **First-touch deploy**
  Deploy the smart account and perform the action together; don’t make “create wallet” a separate step.

## Status & errors (what to surface)

- **Submitted**: we’ve sent your operation to the network.
- **Processing**: simulation and bundling in progress (can take a few seconds).
- **Included**: on-chain; show explorer link.
- **Couldn’t process**: explain plainly:
  - Budget not sponsored (try again with token gas or native fee).
  - Invalid inputs / simulation failed (adjust amounts/slippage).
  - Rate/budget limits reached (try later or reduce size).
